## Getting Started

Welcome to the AerLeaf's BlueBoy Adventure:

What you can do :-

Fight monster,
Get Treasure,
Buy or item,
etc...

How to play :-

Press Enter to attack/confirm/interact
Press C to guard
Press W/A/S/D to move
Press P to Pause
Press E to get inventory
Press "Esc" to get options
